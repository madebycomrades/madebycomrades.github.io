Flexibility

We’re not a company, we’re a group of freelancers that work well together. We can self organise to form a ready-made dev team, or join an existing team of developers. You can hire some of us, or all of us depending on your requirements.

We all care deeply about the quality of the work we produce, and with years of industry experience between us we have the skills to get things done. We are keen advocates of agile working methodologies, testing, best practice and a pragmatic common sense approach to producing high quality work.

Services
